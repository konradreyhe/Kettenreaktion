## Top 10 Takeaways

### 1. No direct competitor exists
No daily physics chain reaction puzzle game exists in the browser space. Kettenreaktion occupies an **uncontested niche** at the intersection of Wordle's daily format and physics puzzle gameplay.

### 2. GIF/MP4 replay is the killer viral feature
Physics chain reactions are inherently "oddly satisfying" content. **Visual replays are more shareable than emoji grids** because failed attempts are entertaining and each solution looks unique. Use `canvas-record` (npm) for hardware-accelerated MP4 via WebCodecs. This is the #1 priority feature for virality.

### 3. Vercel Hobby prohibits commercial use
**Critical blocker.** Any monetization (ads, donations, subscriptions) violates Vercel Hobby terms. Recommendation: migrate to **Cloudflare Pages** (free, unlimited bandwidth, no commercial restrictions) before any monetization.

### 4. German legal requirements are mandatory
**Impressum** and **Datenschutzerklärung** are required even for hobby projects. TTDSG fines up to €300,000. If using only localStorage (no tracking/ads), no cookie banner is needed.

### 5. Chain reaction "juice" is the core differentiator
Escalating feedback (particles, screen shake, pitch-rising sounds, glow) mapped to chain length makes the game *feel* amazing. Phaser 3.90 has 14 built-in WebGL FX effects ready to use. A `getChainFeedback(chainLength)` system should scale all effects together.

### 6. Matter.js determinism is not guaranteed cross-device
Floating-point physics vary across devices. **Never re-simulate replays** — always export as GIF/MP4. Use seeded PRNG for puzzle generation, tolerance-based scoring for fairness.

### 7. Phaser 3.90 is the right choice (Phaser 4 can wait)
Phaser 4 exists (Nov 2024) but maintains API compatibility with v3. Migration will be low-effort when the time comes. No reason to switch now.

### 8. Quick-win features with highest impact
| Feature | Effort | Impact |
|---------|--------|--------|
| Slow-motion replay | S | High — wow factor, shareability |
| Ghost of previous attempt | M | High — learning between attempts |
| Challenge a friend via URL | S | High — viral loop |
| Colorblind palettes + shape markers | S | High — ethical, 8% of male players |
| Near-miss indicator | S | High — turns frustration into learning |
| Global placement heatmap | M | High — social data visualization |
| Streak freeze (Duolingo model) | S | High — prevents #1 cause of abandonment |

### 9. Monetization roadmap (by DAU)
- **0–1K DAU:** Ko-fi donations, zero cost (Cloudflare Pages free)
- **1K DAU:** Banner ads (~$40-100/mo)
- **1K–10K DAU:** Kettenreaktion+ subscription tier
- **10K+ DAU:** Gaming ad networks, media partnerships (German newspapers)
- **100K+ DAU:** Direct ad sales, licensing, app stores

### 10. Launch strategy
Build in public 6 months before launch. Seed micro-influencers 3-4 weeks before. Launch simultaneously on Reddit (r/WebGames), Product Hunt, and press outreach. Track K-factor (share-to-new-player conversion) as north star metric. Use Umami (self-hosted, cookie-free) for analytics.
